### Glossary
- **Full-stack AI**: Integrated control across hardware, software platforms (e.g., CUDA), data infrastructure, energy systems, and applications forming a complete computing environment.
- **Export controls**: Government restrictions on the sale or transfer of advanced technologies, such as AI chips, to specific countries for national security reasons.
- **AI ecosystem**: The interconnected network of compute power, models, data, networks, and policy frameworks enabling AI development and deployment.
- **Systemic governance**: Regulatory and strategic approaches addressing entire technological systems rather than isolated components.
- **Piecemeal regulation**: Fragmented policies targeting single elements (e.g., chips) without coordinating broader ecosystem impacts.
