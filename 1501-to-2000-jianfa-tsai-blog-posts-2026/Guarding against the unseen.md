# Guarding against the unseen

Martial arts and military strategy: To make the simple powerful. Don’t be rigid and inflexible. Be ever-changing and adaptable. Create new, original content tailored to the circumstances. It’s difficult to know that an evil heart lies behind a kind smiling face. It’s hard to guard against conspiracies, tricks, and traps. Learn and apply your knowledge flexibly. The enemy cannot counter you if you control the environment that your enemy resides in, and you do not exist in the same space as your opponent. Do not repeat the same move consecutively or from previous sets. Always create and execute new permutations. Enemies can exploit the gaps in your knowledge and skills when you are on leave or on holiday. Each of your moves is directed to the enemy’s vitals to force the opponent to retreat and defend. Therefore, the enemy is unable to attack you or your allies. Anticipate the enemy’s moves. Advance without retreat. Every move is an attack. Your enemy can only retreat. Then you don’t waste resources on guarding yourself (雷霆枪王, 2026).
