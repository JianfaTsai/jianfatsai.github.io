Peer-reviewed studies note cultural variations: in collectivist societies, relational emphasis may amplify, while individualistic ones risk isolation (Subhan, 2023). Bias in Stoic sources—primarily authored by privileged males—raises questions of applicability to marginalized groups facing daily survival threats. Long-term, overemphasis on death could detract from proactive life-building, per some psychological critiques.

- **Mortality Salience**: Psychological awareness of one's eventual death, often studied in terror management theory.
