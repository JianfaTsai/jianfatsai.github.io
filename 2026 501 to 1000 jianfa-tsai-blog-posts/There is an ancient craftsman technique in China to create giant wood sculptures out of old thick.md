There is an ancient craftsman technique in China to create giant wood sculptures out of old thick tress with magnificent inner wood grains. Thus the "spare parts/waste with beautiful wooden grains" from the wood sculpting, can be used to create beautiful patterns as part of kitchen or samurai knife handles. Separately, consider using pyrography to create intricate burning patterns on the wooden handles. This technique can be used to create custom message desired by the customer via an app or website similar to Etsy and other custom baseball cap design portals. This will create a secondary industry where customers send their old knives in to have the old blade removed so they can keep the handle (memento) without the blade as its a gift from a deceased loved ones, or have a new blade inserted in the old beloved handle (give it new life).
