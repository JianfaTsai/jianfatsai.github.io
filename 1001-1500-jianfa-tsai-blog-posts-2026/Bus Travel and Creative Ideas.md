# Bus Travel and Creative Ideas
### Jianfa Tsai’s Input
Take the bus occasionally to spark creative ideas. Why do I have ideas when I am travelling on the bus?
### SuperGrok AI Analysis
Bus travel sparks creative ideas through mind wandering that facilitates incubation (Baird et al., 2012, https://doi.org/10.1177/0956797612446024). Mild distraction from routine allows unconscious processing during low-demand activity (Ritter & Dijksterhuis, 2014, https://doi.org/10.3389/fnhum.2014.00215). New sensory input breaks habitual patterns, enhancing cognitive flexibility akin to multicultural exposure effects (Maddux et al., 2009).
