# “What exactly is this question asking me to do? What is being asked? What is given? What relationships or judgments can I infer from this given information?   
  
**Organise the information instead of wandering through it randomly. **  
  
**Don’t get distracted by irrelevant details as you read. Map the relationship instead of just guessing. **  
  
**Real intelligence is not about knowing more facts, but in identifying which facts truly matter to the context.”**
