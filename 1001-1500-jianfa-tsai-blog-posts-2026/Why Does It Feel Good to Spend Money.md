# Why Does It Feel Good to Spend Money?

### Jianfa Tsai's Input
Why does it feel good to spend money?

### SuperGrok AI Analysis
Spending money feels good because it triggers dopamine release in the brain's reward centers, creating pleasure and reducing residual sadness via purchase decisions (Rick et al., 2014, https://doi.org/10.1016/j.jcps.2013.12.004).
