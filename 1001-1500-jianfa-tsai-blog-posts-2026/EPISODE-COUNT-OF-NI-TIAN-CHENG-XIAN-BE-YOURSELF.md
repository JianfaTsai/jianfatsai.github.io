### Detailed Overview and Nuances

This production follows the popular "counterattack" (_nixi_) trope prevalent in Chinese digital media. Unlike traditional long-form television, these dramas are designed for vertical viewing on mobile platforms like **DramaBox, ReelShort, or TikTok/Douyin**.

- **Structure and Pacing:** Because each episode is extremely short, the narrative moves at a breakneck pace. The "face-slapping" (_da lian_) mechanics—where the protagonist is underestimated only to reveal overwhelming power—occur frequently to maintain high viewer retention (Lin & Zhao, 2024).
    
- **The "Be Yourself" Title Confusion:** It is important to note that a well-known 24-episode drama from 2021 starring Shen Yue also carries the English title _Be Yourself_. However, that is a youth/campus romance. The drama you've described is a **Fantasy/Xuanhuan** short drama.
    
- **Availability:** These dramas are typically released in a "vertical" format. While the total count is **100 episodes**, they are often compiled into a single long video (approx. 2 hours) on platforms like YouTube for international audiences.
