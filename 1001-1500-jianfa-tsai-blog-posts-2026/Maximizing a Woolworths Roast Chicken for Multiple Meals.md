# Maximizing a Woolworths Roast Chicken for Multiple Meals
### Jianfa Tsai's Input
> How do you get a whole roasted chicken from Woolworths supermarket, Australia, to last you for as many meals as possible? One serve for an adult male of average weight and appetite.
### Authors
Authored by Jianfa Tsai in collaboration with Gemini AI
### Summary
To stretch a single Woolworths roast chicken for an adult male, you can achieve **5 to 7 meals** by immediately portioning the meat and using the carcass to create a nutrient-dense stock for soups or risotto.
### ELI5
Think of the chicken like a LEGO set: eat the big pieces first, then break the leftovers into tiny bits to build new meals like stir-fries and sandwiches, and finally melt down the "scraps" (bones) to make a big pot of soup.
### Strategy for Maximum Yield
To maximize the chicken, you must transition from eating "meat as a main" to "meat as an ingredient." For an adult male, a standard protein serving is approximately **125g to 150g** (roughly the size of a deck of cards) (Low Histamine Eats, 2023).

### Counter-Arguments
While stretching a chicken provides excellent value, it is important to consider nutritional density. Relying on high-carb fillers (bread, rice, pasta) to stretch a small amount of protein can lead to lower satiety levels for an active adult male compared to a high-protein meal (SBS Food, 2023). Additionally, food safety is a concern; shredded chicken should be consumed within 3–4 days or frozen immediately to prevent bacterial growth.
