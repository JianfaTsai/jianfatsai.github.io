Creativity is multi-dimensional. Ask yourself what other options you have not considered? Consider other possibilities. Open yourself up to other cultures, other civilisations and other periods, to how the opposite gender thinks, to how people from other cultures think. It expands your horizon, and then you will be able to make all of these interesting connections between them. Consider what type of things you let into your material (Greene, 2026). It might be more impactful if we rephrase "how women think" to "how the opposite gender thinks". This outreach to a greater number of people.
