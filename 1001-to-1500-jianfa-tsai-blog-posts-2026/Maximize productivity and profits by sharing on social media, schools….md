Maximize productivity and profits by sharing on social media, schools, and universities organically and on YouTube to implement a free productivity hack. Users install a habit in themselves before they step away due to interruption or for breaks. They use the mouse scroll wheel to scroll the previously read paragraphs and sentences up to hide them from the active window view on the computer. That way, when they return, the top left corner or the first sentence displayed on the active window on the computer is the first unread sentence.
