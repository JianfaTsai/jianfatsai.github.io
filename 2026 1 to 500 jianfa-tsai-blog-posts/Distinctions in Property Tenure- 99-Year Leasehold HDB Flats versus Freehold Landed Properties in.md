**Glossary**  
- **HDB Flats**: Public housing apartments built and sold by Singapore's Housing and Development Board, designed for affordable homeownership.  
- **99-Year Lease**: A fixed-term ownership right to use and occupy property for 99 years, after which control reverts to the state.  
- **Freehold Landed Properties**: Detached, semi-detached, or terraced houses with perpetual ownership of both the building and underlying land.  
- **Landed Properties**: Low-rise residential units with direct ground access, as opposed to high-rise apartments.  
- **Leasehold**: Temporary property rights granted for a specified period, common in government-influenced developments.
