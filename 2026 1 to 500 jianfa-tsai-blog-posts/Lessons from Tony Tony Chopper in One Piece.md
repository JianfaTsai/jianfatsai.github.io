**Background Information:**  
Tony Tony Chopper was born a reindeer with a distinctive blue nose on Drum Island leading to rejection by his herd and later by humans after consuming the Human-Human Devil Fruit.  


He was mentored by the quack doctor Hiriluk, who instilled dreams of curing all diseases and the value of a Jolly Roger as a symbol of hope, before being trained in real medicine by Doctor Kureha.  


Chopper joins the Straw Hat Pirates during the Drum Island arc, becoming their doctor and evolving from a timid outcast to a loyal crew member who grows in confidence after the timeskip.
