# App Subscription Management Template

**App Subscription Details to Note**  
- App name  
- Website  
- User account email  
- Phone number  
- Yearly recurring cost  
- Merchant name (as shown in bank app transaction)  

**Purpose**  
Record these details to prevent confusion over bank charges from the subscription.  

**Additional Management Tips**  
- Search by the charged amount across your email and cloud storage for any unknown transactions.  
- Save the yearly recurring cost, along with the bank and merchant name, in your password manager under the app entry.  
- Search using the dollar sign ($) in your password manager to quickly review all active subscriptions for an overview and backup.

**Glossary:**  

App Name: The name of the software or service subscribed to.  


Website: Official site for managing the account.  


Merchant Name: The descriptor appearing on banking statements for the transaction.  


Yearly Recurring Cost: Total annual charge including any taxes or fees.  


**Background Information:**  

In the era of numerous digital subscriptions, unrecognised or forgotten charges can lead to financial leakage.  


This template serves as a personal knowledge management tool for financial hygiene.  


**Supportive Reasoning:**  

Recording these details enables rapid verification against bank statements reducing stress from unknown debits.  


Password manager integration provides secure searchable backup.  


**Counter-Arguments:**  

Merchant names may vary due to payment processors potentially complicating searches.  


Yearly costs can change requiring periodic template updates.  


Phone number inclusion raises minor privacy considerations if not essential.  


**Risks:**  

Storing sensitive account details in password managers requires strong security practices.  


Failure to update records could lead to outdated information during disputes.  


**Improvements:**  

Incorporate fields for renewal date cancellation instructions and last charged date.  


Consider using dedicated subscription tracking apps available in Australia.
