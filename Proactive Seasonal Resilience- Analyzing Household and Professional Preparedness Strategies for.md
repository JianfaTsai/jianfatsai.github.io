### Federal, State, or Local Laws in Australia
In Victoria, no mandatory annual servicing exists for private homeowners' split systems or water heaters, but licensing requirements under the Victorian Building Authority mandate qualified technicians for installations and repairs (Victorian Government, 2025). Gas heating must comply with Energy Safe Victoria standards, including carbon monoxide safeguards. Rental properties face phased minimum energy efficiency rules effective March 2027 for heating, cooling, and hot water. Smoke alarms and general property maintenance fall under Residential Tenancies Act obligations. Federal emergency management frameworks (via the National Emergency Management Agency) encourage self-sufficiency stockpiling without prescribing quantities.
