### Glossary:
Tech neck refers to chronic neck and shoulder pain arising from sustained forward head posture during technology use.
Forward head posture describes the anterior displacement of the head relative to the shoulders increasing effective load on cervical structures.
macOS Voice Control denotes the built in accessibility feature enabling complete voice based navigation dictation and interaction with the computer interface.
Wispr Flow represents an AI powered voice dictation application that transcribes speech into polished formatted text across any application with cross device synchronisation.
Live screen mirroring involves real time duplication or extension of a primary computer display to a secondary monitor facilitating seamless workflow continuity.
Standing desk configuration indicates an adjustable height workstation optimised for upright posture during dynamic movement such as pacing.
