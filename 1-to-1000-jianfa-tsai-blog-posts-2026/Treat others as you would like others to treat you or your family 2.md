# Treat others as you would like others to treat you or your family

Your loved ones do not enjoy being corrected. Your well-intentioned words make them feel inadequate. By providing unconditional love, support, respect, and a listening ear, they will feel safe enough to take the first step to consult you with their troubles (李惠心理, 2026).
