# In the USA, business owners can rent property under their name to live in for x days a year and profit from it. True or false? Which law or tax code denotes this (EdmundCavendishHale, 2026)?

**True**, under the Augusta Rule (IRC Section 280A), business owners can rent their personal residence or vacation home to their own business for up to 14 days per year, receive tax-free rental income (not reported), while the business deducts the rent as a legitimate expense.
