## Comprehensive Biography of Sefas Bansah  
**Early Life and Education**  
* Sefas Bansah is the king of the Hohoe Ewe people in Ghana.  
* His grandfather, a wise and enlightened leader, sent him abroad for education to help improve their impoverished tribe.  
* Bansah earned dual degrees in agricultural machinery and automotive mechanics.  
* Despite his qualifications, he found that his skills were not applicable in his underdeveloped home region, prompting him to stay abroad to work and earn money *00:01:30*  
**Life in Germany**  
* Bansah moved to Germany and worked as an automotive mechanic.  
* He established his own car repair shop and married a German woman, with whom he had two children.  
* Although living comfortably in Germany, he never forgot his responsibilities to his people back home.  
* He sent all his earnings back to his tribe to improve their living conditions *00:02:00*
