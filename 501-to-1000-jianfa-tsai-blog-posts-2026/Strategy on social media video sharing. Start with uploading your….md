Strategy on social media video sharing. Start with uploading your shorts video to YouTube, use the free YouTube inbuilt AI feature to auto create captions in the video. Edit the AI captions for accuracy, post the video to YouTube without background music or other distracting clutter. Download the same edited video with AI captions to your device. Save the video from Photos app to Files app. Upload the edited video with captions to your other social media apps. After posting, open the social media website via incognito web browser app, to double check it.
