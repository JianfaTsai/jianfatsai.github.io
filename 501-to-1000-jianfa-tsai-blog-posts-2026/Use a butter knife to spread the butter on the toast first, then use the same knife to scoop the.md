Use a butter knife to spread the butter on the toast first, then use the same knife to scoop the tuna out of its can and spread it on the bread. This way, you save on dishwashing water costs, dishwashing liquid soap costs, depreciation of dishwashing sponge costs, human labour costs, time costs, energy costs, opportunity costs, and reduced emotional, cognitive and physical stressors involved in the unnecessary use of a second butter knife (otherwise used to expel the tuna from its container).
