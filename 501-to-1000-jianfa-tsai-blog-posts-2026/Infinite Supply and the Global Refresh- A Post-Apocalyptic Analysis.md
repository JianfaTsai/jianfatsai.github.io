### ELI5
A man with a seemingly useless power to "reset" objects realizes he can create infinite brand-new supplies from trash, allowing him to save his family, build a fortress, and eventually heal the entire world from a zombie virus.
### The Mechanism of the "Refresh" Ability
The narrative centers on Linfan, a survivor initially dismissed due to his "F-rank" ability called **Refresh** [[00:00][1]]. While observers believed the power merely reset an object to its state from a few seconds prior, Linfan discovers the "initial state" refers to the **factory-fresh condition** [[01:06][2]]. This distinction transforms a perceived handicap into the ultimate survival tool.

### Strategic Survival and Combat Applications
Linfan employs a "Utility-First" strategy to navigate the lawless landscape of the apocalypse. He utilizes mundane objects with lethal efficiency; for instance, he uses a **full can of Coca-Cola** as a projectile to stagger enemies before finishing them with a restored fruit knife [[04:11][13]].
To maintain security, he practices **Invisible Logistics**, stashing his massive surplus of supplies in hidden sewers to avoid detection during inspections at survivor strongholds like Blue Bay [[05:21][14]]. This allows him to maintain the appearance of a "frail refugee" while possessing the resources of a god [[06:09][15]].

### From Personal Survival to Global Restoration
As Linfan's power evolves, the scope of his influence shifts from micro-management to macro-restoration.
1. **The Laboratory Phase:** He discovers he can cure the zombie virus by refreshing the human body to its pre-infection state [[31:44][16]].
2. **The Power Nullification:** He learns to "refresh" the power cores of rival ability users, effectively resetting them to normal humans to neutralize threats without killing [[29:40][17]].
3. **The Global Refresh:** In a climactic event, he expands his range to the entire horizon, refreshing the earth itself—restoring fertile soil, clean air, and turning millions of zombies back into humans [[41:51][18]].
