### Facts
Chua Lam was born August 18, 1941, in Singapore to Chaozhou ancestry, studied in Japan, joined Shaw Brothers in Hong Kong, produced films, hosted television programs, authored over 150 books on food, travel, and life, served as consultant for *A Bite of China*, and built wealth through diversified creative enterprises before passing on June 25, 2025 (Cai, 2025; Wikipedia contributors, 2026).
