The strategy may inadvertently encourage over-purchasing, conflicting with evidence-based calls for precise, needs-based shopping.

### Glossary
- **Bulk Purchasing**: Acquiring multiple units of a good in one transaction to reduce future acquisitions.
- **Intergenerational Efficiency**: Resource strategies designed to benefit not only the current user but also descendants through habit or inheritance.
- **Perishability**: The tendency of food items, such as dairy, to spoil over time despite proper storage.
