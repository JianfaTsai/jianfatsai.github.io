"Website wranglers" is a colloquial term for web designers, developers, or content managers who specialize in handling, fixing, and maintaining websites—often resolving frustrating technical issues. The term implies a proactive, "hands-on" approach to managing WordPress, content strategy, or technical SEO, acting as a "nerd brain on tap" for site owners. Key aspects of "website wranglers" include:  
* **Problem-Solving:** They handle technical issues (like fixing "rage-Googling" moments), ensuring smooth functionality, such as moving chatbots or updating site layouts.  
* **Content Management:** They focus on restructuring site architecture, optimizing user experience (UX), and managing content strategy for businesses.  
* **WordPress Expertise:** Often used by bloggers or developers managing WordPress templates and site design.  
* **Content Design:** They are involved in deciding how to structure and name sections of a website for better user engagement.
