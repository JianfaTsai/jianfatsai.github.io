### Counter-Arguments & Limitations
While NotebookLM is a powerful tool for information synthesis, a critical counter-argument is that it may encourage "surface-level" engagement with texts. If students rely solely on AI-generated summaries and podcasts, they may miss the nuanced "slow-reading" required to master complex cataloging standards or ethical information frameworks. Furthermore, the video notes that it does not yet automatically sync with Google Docs [[13:23][11]], meaning any updates to your library research notes must be manually re-uploaded.
