Improve profits by improving the learning by solving the teaching and student problem in webinars, live or recorded, where the teacher reads the slides a lot, but the student has to waste time and eyeball to find out which part of the 20 words suddenly displayed on the screen the teacher is reading the slide on each word. The teacher can use, for example, a mouse pointer to read and red underline the first word of each sentence that he reads aloud on the slide. That way, it can be used as a pointer to communicate to the student, especially high school students, where he reads from point to point; once he stops underlining, he will be communicating his other parts of the teaching.
