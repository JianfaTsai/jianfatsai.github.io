Single pot pasta cooking method:  
1. Cook the broccoli.  
2. Boil it; scoop it up.  
3. Cook the pasta; boil it; scoop it up.  
4. Drain the water; dry it with a paper towel.  
5. Add cooking oil into the pot; pot-fry the chicken with the lid on. Drain off excess oil.   
6. Add the pasta sauce.  
7. Add the noodles and broccoli in.
