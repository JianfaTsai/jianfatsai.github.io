### Action Steps
1. Review your university/PhD handbook and consult your supervisor to confirm non-sensitive book criteria, documenting approval for archival purposes.
2. Join the Amazon Associates program via their website, verifying Australian eligibility and setting up your storefront with book categories.
3. Create a dedicated "Book Reviews" playlist in YouTube Studio, uploading 1-2 pilot videos excluding any PhD-related topics.
4. Research and generate affiliate links using Amazon's SiteStripe tool, pasting them into video descriptions with mandatory ACL-compliant disclosure text (e.g., "As an Amazon Associate, I earn from qualifying purchases").
5. Design custom thumbnails in Canva (1280x720 px) featuring the book cover, your expressive face, bold title/author text, and high-contrast hooks for optimal CTR.
6. Optimize video metadata: Add timestamps, end screens linking to the playlist, and pinned comments reinforcing affiliate transparency.
7. Monitor performance in YouTube Analytics for the first month, adjusting based on engagement while maintaining disclosure logs for compliance.
8. Evaluate quarterly: Assess commissions, learning gains, and community feedback; refine to ensure ongoing win-win alignment without institutional risks.
