For Your Library Australia website, libraries managed by Knox City council, Maroondah city council and Yarra Ranges city council, which library or suburb is nearest to my home at Dowling Road, Oakleigh South, VIC Australia?  
  
**📚 Nearest Your Library branch: Knox Library in Wantirna South suburb.** (approx. 10-13 km drive).  
  
Your Library (full name: Your Library Ltd) is the cooperative public library service operated jointly by Knox City Council, Maroondah City Council, and Yarra Ranges Shire Council in Melbourne's eastern suburbs. It provides free access to books, digital resources, events, and facilities across 13 branches plus mobile libraries and reading rooms.  
  
Oakleigh South (postcode 3167) lies in the City of Monash, outside these three councils. The closest Your Library branches therefore belong to Knox City Council. Knox Library (also called Ngarrgoo or Knox Library at Wantirna South) sits at 425 Burwood Highway, Wantirna South VIC 3152. Road distance from central Oakleigh South/Dowling Road area is roughly 10-13 km (about 15-25 minutes by car depending on traffic and exact starting point along Dowling Road). Public transport options exist via buses (e.g., line 693) or combinations with trains, though driving or rideshare is usually quickest.
