As you transfer the paraphrased words from a paragraph from the PDF onto your assignment document, highlight the paragraph on the PDF so you know that you have already reviewed it. That way, when you go back to the PDF, at a glance you can see the paragraph is not highlighted to make sure that you didn't miss out on anything. Triple click on a sentence or paragraph to highlight the entire paragraph or sentence. Assign a physical keyboard key on Apple Magic Keyboard numpad to highlight the Word document paragraph. You can assign three keys to highlight in three different colors.
