What does the licence plate for a white BMW car in Singapore: SKK 9181M symbolise?  
  
**The SKK 9181M plate on the white BMW in Singapore symbolizes good fortune and prosperity.**  
  
SKK is just a standard private car series (started ~2013). The lucky part is the number **9181**:  
  
- In Hokkien/Mandarin sounds: **9-1-8** = **"jiu yao fa" (就要发)** → "will soon prosper" or "about to get rich".    
- The extra **1** often adds "surely" or "one" for emphasis.  
  
This is a popular choice in Singapore for "huat" (prosper) vibes. Nothing special about the white BMW or exact plate — it's the numbers that carry the meaning.
