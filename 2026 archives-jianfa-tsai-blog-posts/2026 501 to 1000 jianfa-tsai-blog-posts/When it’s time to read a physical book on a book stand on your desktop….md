# When it’s time to read a physical book on a book stand on your desktop computer table, move the Magic Keyboard to the left edge of the table (angled vertically). Move the book stand back to the keyboard's previous position and use an iPad Pro with an iPad keyboard case or a MacBook to take notes, research, look up terms, or use Wispr Flow to voice dictate notes into digital text from the physical book. You can capitalise on the floor space (under your table (sides)) to put your iPad, or a book stand, once you have completed the session.
