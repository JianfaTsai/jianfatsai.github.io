# Hang Whiteboard On Door With Over The Door Hooks

![Hang Whiteboard On Door With Over The Door Hooks][image-1]

Integrate over-the-door hooks with whiteboards to hang whiteboards on the inside- and outside-facing parts of wardrobe and bedroom doors, maximising global insight creation and profits. X-Protector brand (from Amazon Australia) thick foam pad on the back of the whiteboard to dampen noise from the whiteboard hitting the hard surface of the door.
