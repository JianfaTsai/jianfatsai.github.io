# Journey into the mind of Australia's most notorious outlaw  
  
In the multi-award-winning web experience Mouthful of Dust, explore Ned Kelly's Jerilderie Letter in stunning detail through 3D scans while author Nam Le reimagines the day Kelly dictated the letter to fellow gang member Joe Byrne.
