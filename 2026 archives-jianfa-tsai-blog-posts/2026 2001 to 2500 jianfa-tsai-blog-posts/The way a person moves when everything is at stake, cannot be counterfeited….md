# The way a person moves when everything is at stake, cannot be counterfeited.   
  
Be defined by not what you fight, but by what you protect.   
  
Plan decisions across years and decades, calibrates what each person needs to hear in order to make the choice they were always going to make,   
  
Every word she speaks is a calculated move in a game played across multiple lifetimes.  
  
Communicate accurate information for others to survive what is coming.  
  
Respect the person being guided with the right amount of truth that is appropriate to them at that point of time, after considering their current development.  
  
Identify someone’s calibre by testing how they perform under pressure.   
  
What do you do with freedom when you discover you have it?  
  
Say nothing that doesn’t need saying.  
  
Protect that which matters most.  
  
The best protection is nobody knows that you exist.
