Mechanical device to auto cut off the power of the wall power outlet based on a mechanical timer system. That way, you can ensure or increase the safety levels by cutting off the power to charge your phone and pods and watch every night in case you forget to turn off the power. Reduce time waste and reduce hassle, and the device auto turns on the power in the morning when you wake up, for example at 7:30 p.m. That way, you wouldn't have to remember or forget to turn on the power to charge your devices left with low battery levels, which could be a safety hazard later on.
