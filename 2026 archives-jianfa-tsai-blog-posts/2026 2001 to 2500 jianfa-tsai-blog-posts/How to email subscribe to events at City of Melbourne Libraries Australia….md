How to email subscribe to events at City of Melbourne Libraries Australia?  
  
**To subscribe by email to City of Melbourne Libraries events and programs:**  
  
1. Go to the official Libraries page: [https://www.melbourne.vic.gov.au/libraries](https://www.melbourne.vic.gov.au/libraries).  
  
2. Click the **Subscribe** link for the **City of Melbourne Libraries newsletter** (monthly updates on programs and events).  
  
**Direct subscribe link:** [https://confirmsubscription.com/h/j/612B8F8A84678D79](https://confirmsubscription.com/h/j/612B8F8A84678D79).
