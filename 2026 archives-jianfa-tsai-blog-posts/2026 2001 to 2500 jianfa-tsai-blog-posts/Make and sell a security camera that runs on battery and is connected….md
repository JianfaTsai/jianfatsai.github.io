Make and sell a security camera that runs on battery and is connected to a wall power outlet with a built-in light. Before the user leaves his bedroom, he presses a button on the security camera or via the security camera app to turn on the camera in motion sensor or sentry mode. If there's any motion by object, animal, or human (or just human only), the light on the physical security camera will flash a certain colour. Next time, when the user returns back to his bedroom, he can check the non-WiFi security camera to see if there's any physical flashing of the light to determine whether there's any motion detected or not, or an unauthorised entry to his bedroom.
