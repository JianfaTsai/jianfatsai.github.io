**In macOS Finder, press Option + Up Arrow (⌥ + ↑).**    
  
This jumps to the top of the list and selects the first file.    
  
- Works in **List view** (best), Column view, and Gallery view.    
- Use **Option + Down Arrow (⌥ + ↓)** for the last file.    
  
(If you have an extended keyboard, the **Home** key does the same.)    
  
No other shortcuts needed.
