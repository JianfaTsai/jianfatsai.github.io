Under the Privacy Act 1988 (Cth), unauthorized access to or interference with personal information, including inferred profiles from transcripts, constitutes a breach of Australian Privacy Principles 11, triggering mandatory notification for eligible data breaches (Office of the Australian Information Commissioner, 2025). State laws, such as Victoria’s Crimes Act 1958, provide supplementary provisions for computer offenses without overriding federal cybercrime frameworks.
