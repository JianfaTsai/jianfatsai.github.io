Counter-hacking on temporary after you have finalised a document for work or for legal in a Word document: save it as a PDF and print out the PDF on physical paper by a printer before you submit the document. Scan the physical printout of the document and upload the scan image of that document into AI. Upload your final version before submission as a digital Word document as the second attachment into AI and ask AI to compare the two documents and tell you the differences. This way you'll be able to know if there are any unauthorised changes due to hacking or tampering.
