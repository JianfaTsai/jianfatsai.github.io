# Best PDF annotation tools for Mac  
  
****PDF Expert** is the best PDF annotation tool for Mac (especially for academic research).**  
  
**It’s fast, native, and packed with precise tools: highlight, underline, sticky notes, shapes, freehand, and AI summarization—perfect for marking up papers. Works seamlessly with Obsidian/Zotero workflows.**  
  
****Quick tip**: One-time purchase (often on sale) or subscription; pairs great with your Obsidian setup.**  
  
****Simpler/free alternatives**:**  
**- **Skim** (free): Lightweight, made for scientific papers—highlights, notes, snapshots, easy export. Academic favorite.**  
**- **Highlights** (free tier): Research-focused; auto-extracts annotations into exportable notes (direct to Obsidian).**  
**- **Preview** (built-in): Basic markup—good enough for quick stuff.**
