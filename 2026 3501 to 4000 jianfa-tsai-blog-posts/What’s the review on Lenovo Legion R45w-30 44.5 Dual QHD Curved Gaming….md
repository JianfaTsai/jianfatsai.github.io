Glossary:  
Dual QHD denotes a 5120 by 1440 resolution that effectively doubles standard QHD horizontally for an ultrawide 32 to 9 aspect ratio.  
VA panel refers to Vertical Alignment technology that delivers superior native contrast ratios compared with IPS panels though with potential trade-offs in viewing angles and response times.  
FreeSync Premium Pro constitutes AMD’s adaptive synchronisation technology that eliminates screen tearing while supporting high dynamic range content.  
True Split mode enables the monitor to function as two independent displays within a single panel for enhanced productivity without physical dual-monitor setups.  
KVM switch indicates a keyboard video mouse function that allows seamless control of multiple computers from one input device set.  
Background Information:  
Released in late 2023 the Lenovo Legion R45w-30 (model 67B1GAC3AU in Australia) targets gamers and professionals seeking an affordable super-ultrawide alternative to premium offerings such as the Samsung Odyssey G9 series.  
The monitor features a 44.5-inch VA panel with 1500R curvature, 450 nits typical brightness, 3000 to 1 static contrast ratio, and DisplayHDR 400 certification.  
Connectivity includes two HDMI 2.1 TMDS ports, DisplayPort 1.4, USB-C with 75 W power delivery, Gigabit Ethernet, and a built-in USB hub.  
Australian retailers such as JB Hi-Fi and Amazon AU stock the unit with pricing historically ranging from approximately 999 to 1100 AUD depending on promotions.

Counter Arguments:  
Critics note that default out-of-box image quality appears soft and washed-out relative to competitors necessitating professional calibration for optimal results.  
The bulky stand footprint demands significant desk depth which may prove impractical in compact workspaces.  
HDR400 certification yields only entry-level high dynamic range performance with limited brightness peaks and colour accuracy in HDR mode.  
Overclocking to 170 Hz provides negligible real-world benefit and resets automatically after periods of inactivity.  
Built-in speakers deliver adequate volume yet lack bass depth rendering them unsuitable for immersive audio without external supplementation.  
At full retail price the monitor faces stiff competition from dropping prices on OLED ultrawides that offer superior contrast and colour vibrancy.352128
