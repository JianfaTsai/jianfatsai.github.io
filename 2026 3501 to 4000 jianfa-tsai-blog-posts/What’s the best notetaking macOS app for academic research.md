# What’s the best notetaking macOS app for academic research?  
  
**Obsidian** is the best note-taking macOS app for academic research.  
  
It’s free (core app), stores everything as local Markdown files (no lock-in, future-proof), and shines with bidirectional links, graph view for connecting ideas/papers, and plugins for Zotero citations, PDF annotation, and querying notes. Perfect for literature reviews, linking concepts, and building a personal knowledge base.  
  
**Quick setup tip**: Pair it with Zotero for references and iCloud/Dropbox for sync.  
  
**Simpler alternatives**:  
- **Apple Notes**: Built-in, fast, AI features—good for basics.  
- **Notion**: Strong for databases and structure, but cloud-based with limits.  
  
Obsidian wins for most researchers due to its depth and flexibility on Mac. Download at obsidian.md.
