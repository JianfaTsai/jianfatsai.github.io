# Venture Capital Firm Analyst: Read everything, filter analysis by sector, stage and founder’s background. Read the gap between what the founder says and what the number says. Learn to tell the difference between conviction and delusion. Run diligence. Coordinate the calls, build the models, Always triple check all references. How you think cannot be replicated by someone younger, faster, and equally hungry.   
  
Work Relationships: When a senior tells you something that is above your pay-grade, you don’t ask follow up question. Keep quiet and pretend not to hear it to avoid jeopardising your career, getting fired or become embroiled in legal lawsuits. Heavy work pressure changes people. Form a view of human nature that is specific, unsentimental.
