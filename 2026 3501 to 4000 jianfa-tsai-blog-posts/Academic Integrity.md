## Academic Integrity  
Your responses must:  
• Be written in your own words  
• Include sources if you reference external material  
• Demonstrate your own understanding  
Plagiarism or copying may result in an unsatisfactory result.  
Tips for Success  
• Read the policies carefully  
• Use library websites as evidence  
• Take notes while researching  
• Structure answers clearly  
• Explain ideas in your own words
