### Evidence

FSANZ guidance recommends minimizing over-browning as a precautionary measure without asserting definitive risk (Food Standards Australia New Zealand, 2023). Practical evidence from van life communities supports edible-plate methods for reducing water use and waste, though no large-scale controlled trials exist specifically for mobile contexts.
