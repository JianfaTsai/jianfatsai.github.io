**Glossary:**  
* **eSIM**: Embedded Subscriber Identity Module — a programmable digital SIM built into the device hardware, activated remotely without a physical card.  
* **Physical SIM**: Removable nano-SIM card inserted into a device tray, containing subscriber data in hardware form.  
* **SIM Swap Fraud**: Unauthorised transfer of a mobile number to a new SIM (physical or eSIM) by impersonating the account holder, often to intercept SMS-based authentication.  
* **IMEI**: International Mobile Equipment Identity — unique device identifier that binds eSIM profiles.  
* **Find My**: Apple’s location tracking service that continues functioning with an active eSIM even if the device is powered off in some cases.
