**Glossary**  
* **Dual QHD (DQHD)**: 5120 x 1440 resolution, equivalent to two 2560 x 1440 displays side-by-side without a bezel.  
* **PPI (Pixels Per Inch)**: Measure of pixel density; higher values yield sharper text and images at typical viewing distances. Approximately 109 PPI on the 49-inch model.  
* **IPS Black**: Advanced IPS panel with improved contrast ratio (up to 2000:1) compared to standard IPS, enhancing text readability on white backgrounds.  
* **Curvature (3800R or similar)**: Gentle curve that reduces edge distortion for wide screens.  
* **Scaling**: Operating system adjustment (e.g., 100-150% in Windows/macOS) that balances text size and sharpness.
