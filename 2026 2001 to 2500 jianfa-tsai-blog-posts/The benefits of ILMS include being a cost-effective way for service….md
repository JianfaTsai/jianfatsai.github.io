The benefits of ILMS include being a cost-effective way for service owners to deliver higher service levels to a greater number of citizens. This is done through automation, standardisation to reduce human error, data syncing, and allowing members, staff, and management to self-service various ILMS modules to meet their needs or perform their job roles. As the platform is online, all data is logged, providing a forensic audit trail and accountability, which in turn deters corruption and crime.
