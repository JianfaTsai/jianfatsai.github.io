# And here is the basic framework (examples are covered  
# in the livestream itself):  
# When you are learning anything new or encounter any  
# new problem, engage the problem (1) analytically, (2)  
# creatively, and (3) practically.  
# 1. Key Analytical Questions:  
# What exactly is the problem? (one sentence only)  
# What are the 3 most important variables affecting it?  
# What evidence do I actually have vs what am I  
# assuming?  
# 2. Key Creative Questions:  
**What if the opposite were true?**  
**What would someone completely different from me do?**  
**What's a solution I would normally dismiss?**  
**3. Key Practical Questions:**  
**What is the smallest action I can take in the next 24**  
**hours?**  
**What would stop me from doing it?**  
**How do I remove that obstacle right now?**
