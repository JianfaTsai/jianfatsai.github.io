# The elderly with poor eyesight can’t read tiny text on food packaging, so they end up cooking semi-raw food. Help them by creating a phone camera app or a home station with multiple cameras and built-in speakers that can use AI to scan the front of food packaging for the brand name and product name. The station or phone speakers will read aloud the product brand name, product food name, weight, expiry date, and cooking instructions. Separately, advise the elderly not to buy new food brands or cook new dishes due to their failing eyesight and declining cognitive capacity.
