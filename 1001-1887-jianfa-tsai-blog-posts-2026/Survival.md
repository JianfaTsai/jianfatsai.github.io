Survival: 
1. Find an isolated island that’s not near any mainland and surrounded by vast ocean. Island is not on any fault line, outside major storm belts, and had a natural flat area (perfect for building an airstrip). Island is surrounded by mountains that could serve as natural cover. 
2. Build narrow passages where only 1 person can squeeze through at a time to allow reduced number of bodyguards to be able to fend off armed attackers. 
3. Create maze passageways, secret rooms and hidden tunnels. 
4. If one passage gets blown up, you can use other passages to get back to the surface. 
5. Energy supply is key. Install wind turbines, tidal and nuclear power generators. 
6. Water. Set up rainwater collectors, underground water purification systems, and seawater desalination plants. Further process the water with high temperature, cold temperature, ultraviolet and chemical sterilisation. 
7. Waste water treatment system to handle sewage and extract clean water out of sewage. The water may not be drinkable, but can be used for other purposes e.g. feeding animals, plants or use as weights. Although research needs to be done on long term effects on humans that consume the animal meat or plants that drinks from processed water that may contain micro contaminants. 
8. Waterproof all walls. 
9. Blast doors sealed airtight when closed and locked. 
10. Install multiple air purification systems.
11. Stockpile ample oxygen canisters.
12. Create multiple self sustaining sealed compartments in the event other areas are contaminated. 
13. Implement latest farming tech. 
14. Stockpile medicine, food and water. 
15. Purchase soldiers, military equipment, reconnaissance drones, military vehicles, and firearms. 
16. Install underwater artificial reefs to deter ships from approaching. 
17. Set up electric fencing encircling the entire coastline.
18. Set up multiple underwater mines.
19. Install multiple radar systems to detect approaching enemies.
20. Set up AI laser auto aiming night vision turrets and long range missile systems. 
21. Create decoys.
22. Control global satellites.
23. Acquire airlines and shipping companies worldwide.
24. Ignore your enemies or frenemies probing questions or light hearted conversations as they seek to mine intel to use what they heard against you and your loved ones.
25. Build electric nets underwater surrounding the nation? Nets can break over time. Ideally, build something that can last for centuries to barricade the underwater part of the island. When constructing underground bunker, don’t forget to dig much deeper to layer with ultra hard concrete than several ultra thick laters of metal as the base to prevent enemies tunneling from underground into your bunker.
26. Use information gaps to fight battles. 
27. Deliberately leak intel to the enemy to lure the enemy to an exposed location so you can take the opponent out (EidolonZero, 2026).
