When you need to buy distilled water (packaged in hard plastic bottles) from the supermarket to water your indoor plants, ask A on how to use Sharpie to mark the exterior of the plastic bottled water to denote each segment that equals X ml (water required to water the plant per session, in consideration of plant consumption rates and ambient water evaporation rates). The water amount overflowing the drainage storage base of the plant pot, thus preventing making a mess and reducing the number of times needed to drain the excess water.
