Sometimes you'll be hit with unexpected bills that come every few years or every 10 years, like your driver's licence renewal that costs a few hundred dollars. Therefore, it's important to set a recurring reminder in your Apple Reminders app to notify you every few years. For example, your licence lasts you 10 years, so remind you nine and a half years later. You have six months to save up to pay for the large sum licence renewal fee.
