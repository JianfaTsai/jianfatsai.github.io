# Keep your transaction account at £0 most of the time to prevent unauthorised charges. Set a recurring reminder to lock your debit and credit cards via the bank app before you sleep. Avoid setting up direct debits for telcos and utilities, as maintaining a balance in your transaction account encourages bad habits like leaving large sums of money there, which criminals may steal or use for fraudulent charges. Reframe card fees for utilities and telco bills as security insurance fees. Set recurring reminders to pay all household and personal bills by card. When you need to visit the mall or make a purchase, transfer only the exact amount just before spending—nothing more.
