# Implementing Daily Reflection Reminders
## User’s Input
Set reminders to notify you three times daily to pause what you are doing, ponder and formulate a question.
## SuperGrok AI Analysis
Daily structured reflection through reminders enhances metacognition and learning outcomes (Lew & Schmidt, 2011). Pausing thrice daily to formulate questions promotes deeper cognitive engagement and professional growth (Larsen et al., 2016).
