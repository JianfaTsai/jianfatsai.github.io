### Glossary:
Pixels Per Inch (PPI) refers to the density of pixels on a display directly influencing text sharpness and eye comfort during prolonged viewing.
Tandem OLED denotes the advanced dual layer organic light emitting diode technology in the iPad Pro M4 delivering exceptional contrast brightness and colour accuracy.
Sidecar is an Apple Continuity feature enabling the iPad to function as an extended display for a Mac computer.
Ultrawide monitor describes a display with an aspect ratio typically of 32:9 or similar providing expansive horizontal workspace equivalent to two standard monitors.
Universal Control permits seamless cursor and input sharing across multiple Apple devices within the same ecosystem.
