**Relevant Federal, State or Local Laws in Australia:**  


Under the Australian Consumer Law (federal) gift cards purchased on or after 1 November 2019 must remain redeemable for at least three years from supply date.  


Expiry must be clearly printed on the card.  


No post-supply fees such as activation, account-keeping, balance enquiry or inactivity charges are permitted.  


In Victoria (Melbourne location) Consumer Affairs Victoria enforces these rules alongside federal ACCC oversight with no additional state-specific gift card restrictions identified.
