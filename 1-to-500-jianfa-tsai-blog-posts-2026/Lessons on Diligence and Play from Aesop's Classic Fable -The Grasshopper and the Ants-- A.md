### Explain Like I’m 5
Imagine a sunny summer day. The busy ant carries crumbs and seeds all day to save food for cold winter. The happy grasshopper sings, dances, and plays without a worry. When winter arrives and snow covers everything, the grasshopper feels hungry and cold with nothing to eat. He asks the ant for help, but the ant says, "You played all summer while I worked—now dance through the winter!" The simple idea is that working hard today helps you stay safe tomorrow, but too much play without planning can leave you in trouble.

### Glossary

- **Play**: Recreational activity done for enjoyment, relaxation, or creativity, without immediate practical purpose.

- **Foresight**: The ability to anticipate future events and plan accordingly.
- **Procrastination**: Delaying tasks that need to be done, often in favor of immediate pleasure.
