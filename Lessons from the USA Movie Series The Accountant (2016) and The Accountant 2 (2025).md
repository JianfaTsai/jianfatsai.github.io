### Problem Statement
The *Accountant* series raises the core problem of how popular cinema represents neurodiversity and forensic accounting ethics while navigating the blurred lines between justice, vigilantism, and legal compliance. In an era of increasing neurodiverse workforce participation, these films highlight untapped talents but risk perpetuating harmful stereotypes, creating a need for balanced critical analysis that informs real-world application without endorsing illegal activities (Jones et al., 2023).
