**Glossary:**

Apple Pay: Secure NFC-based digital wallet using device-specific tokens instead of card numbers. 

Wallet App: iOS native tool for storing debit/credit/transit cards with auto-sync to paired Apple Watch. 

Stranded: Inability to make payments due to lost/stolen device without physical cash backup.
